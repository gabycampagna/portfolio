Armed for Success - Sponsors

Armed for Success is the first of its kind two-day Virtual Web Conference, focusing on supporting military service members and veterans seeking education information. The virtual keynote addresses, workshops and expert presenters will provide the education, financial, and social resources military service members need to succeed in school. The virtual event creates an 'in-person' gathering, without the travel time, expense and hassles for attendees and sponsors, and brings together top military and education leaders.

The event's many features include a Main Exhibit Hall, Education Hall, Resource Library, and Networking Lounge, creating a number of effective sponsorship options sure to catch the attention of military service members globally.

We Have Several Sponsor Opportunities Available:

  • Venue Banners: Banner ads that will grab attendee attention as they wander the Main Exhibit Hall, attend speeches and seminars, and check out the lounge and library.

  • Keynote Sponsorship: The keynote presentation kicking off this major online event presents an attractive opportunity to reach a large and captive audience at the event's onset.

And the weekend's just the beginning of your exposure and outreach! Armed for Success will remain on the web on an 'on-demand' basis for 90 days following the conference, resulting in three months' of additional traffic and promotion.

This is a unique opportunity to extend your market reach, generate highly qualified leads, and gain maximum exposure at a minimum investment. All while doing a great service to the very deserving men and women of the U.S. military.
